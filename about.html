Our journey!

We at preor hated waiting for our food in the restaurant, It takes up so much time just to prepare our favourite dishes and for enjoying it, we have to waste 30-40 mins of our time. That's when we wanted to solve this, and we founded Preor, which is India's first revolutionary food preordering platform that enables users to enjoy their meals without the need of waiting

Preor is a revolutionary new food preordering platform that enables users to preorder their food to save time and skip the need to wait
